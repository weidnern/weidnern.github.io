Nick Weidner

Graduate Research Assistant

University of South Carolina

I am currently pursuing my Masters of Science in computer science at USC after completing my B.S. in computer science and math in the spring of 2016. My primary research interests are computer graphics, computer vision, image processing, and robotics. My masters thesis work focuses on using stereo video of an underwater cave system with limited lighting, and using the stereo image data to generate a geometrically accurate point cloud, construct a wireframe model, and accurately overlay textures to create an interactive environment. I hope to pursue my PhD at another university after completing my masters.
